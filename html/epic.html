Strategic Impact Analysis

Manual Workflow Limitations (SAP)
  • Scheduling: Patient cannot book follow-up appointments for the rest of the week.

  • Documentation: No official proof of visit generated for the patient's employer.

  • Patient Risk: Reliance on patient memory leads to forgotten appointments.

  • Management Gap: Records show only 1 patient despite a whole week of work.
Transformation Benefits (EPIC)
  • Access: Individual appointments scheduled for every single visit phase.

  • Validation: Verified proof of attendance provided for every visit.

  • Data Accuracy: Records accurately reflect 100% of technologist productivity.

  • Workflow: Organized, transparent, and patient-centered protocols.

Deep Dive & Professional Insight

This digital engineering transformation was published as a case study highlighting the strategic optimization of clinical tech workflows. The analysis focuses on leveraging enterprise systems to resolve critical scheduling gaps and enhance patient safety.
