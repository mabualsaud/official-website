CPIP Framework (Validation Phase)

The core architecture utilizes the Clinical Prompt Injection Protocols (CPIP) framework. This specialized validation phase research is explicitly engineered to mitigate clinical hallucinations—specifically targeting instances where generative models prematurely guess a diagnostic or treatment outcome without having necessary patient background context.

Instead of treating interactions as basic conversational chat, CPIP converts the session into a specialized reasoning simulation. This mandates that the AI system pauses, maps hidden data deficiencies, and explicitly requests complex operational indicators (such as exact medical laboratory values, vital arrays, or history baselines) before outputting diagnostic feedback.
